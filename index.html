Score a regatta the easy way

Set up a regatta with the minimum details, add your boats, then tap finishers in the order they cross the line. Scoring follows the US Sailing Racing Rules of Sailing (2025–2028), Appendix A — Low Point System, including penalty scores, discards, and Appendix A8 tie-breaking.

Everything is stored privately in your browser. Use Export to back up or move data.
