Score a one-design regatta the easy way

Built for one-design fleet racing. Just enter boat sail numbers in the order they finish, add a new race, and repeat — the standings score themselves under the US Sailing Racing Rules of Sailing (2025–2028), Appendix A — Low Point System, including penalty scores, throwouts, and Appendix A8 tie-breaking.

Easy starts a pickup regatta with common defaults so you can score immediately — no setup required. Everything is stored privately in your browser; use Export to back up or move data.
